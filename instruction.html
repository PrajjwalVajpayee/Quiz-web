Quiz Instructions

Follow these instructions to get started:

  • Read each question carefully before selecting your answer.
  • There will bw 3 question and for each question there will be options.
  • Select the answer you believe is correct by clicking on the choice.
  • Click the "Next" button to move to the next question.
  • For each correct question you will get +5 marks.
  • If you get a wrong answer you will get -1 mark.
  • Click the "Submit" button to finish the quiz.
